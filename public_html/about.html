About Cuacane

Bridging the gap between complex meteorological data and daily life.

Cuacane is a modern weather application built to help users make better decisions. Whether you are planning a road trip across the city or analyzing climate trends for research, our tools provide the precision you need.

Founded in 2024, our mission is to integrate Artificial Intelligence into standard weather forecasting. By moving beyond simple "Sunny" or "Rainy" labels, we provide risk assessments and route-specific advice.
